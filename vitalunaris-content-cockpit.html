🧭 Decisions we need from the client first

These block correct content everywhere — please confirm before we finalise copy. Each is a real contradiction found inside the current site.

1. Where is the practice located — Zürich or Buchs?
The homepage says “VitaLunaris in Zürich”, but the footer address is Eichenaustrasse 32, CH-9470 Buchs (SG), and an FAQ says “vor Ort in Buchs”. We assume Buchs (SG) is correct and have written copy accordingly — please confirm. (If sessions also happen in Zürich, tell us and we’ll phrase it as “Buchs & online, plus Zürich on request”.)
2. How long is the full Ausbildung — 3 + 3 months, or 18–24 months?
The Level 1 and Level 2 pages each say “3 Monate”, but the Ausbildung overview FAQ says the whole training runs “18 bis 24 Monate”. These can’t both be true. Please give us the real duration of Level 1, Level 2, and the gap between them.
3. What is the real “Praxisangebote” / “Angebote” structure?
The navigation lists Praxisangebote → 1:1 Aufstellungen · Hypnose · Numerologieberatung · Fussreflexzonenbehandlung · Jawort by Jansen. The footer lists a different set under /angebot → Einzelsitzung · Paarsitzung · Familienkonstellation · Retreat. We need one agreed list of services (with prices & duration) before these pages can be written.
4. Real dates, prices, group sizes & the workshop schedule
All dates (“12.8.2026”, “28.6.2026”…), group sizes (“max. 5” vs “max. 9 Teilnehmer”) and some prices are placeholders. We need the real 2026/2027 workshop calendar, the Level 1 / Level 2 start dates, and confirmed prices (currently Level 1 = CHF 4’800, Level 2 = CHF 6’200).
5. Real testimonials
Every quote (“Maria S.”, “Thomas K.”…) is invented placeholder text. Testimonials must be genuine quotes from real clients/participants (with their consent). Our German drafts below only show the right tone & length — please replace them with real voices.

🌐 Site-wide issues (affect many pages)

Fix once, benefits everywhere.

Broken links Internal links that point to pages that don’t exist

  • /kontakt — the #1 priority. Every “Erstgespräch buchen / Jetzt anmelden / Termin buchen” button points here, but the page doesn’t exist. Right now no one can convert.
  • /ueber-uns — linked from many pages (“Mehr über uns”), not built.
  • /angebot & /angebot#… — used on the homepage & footer; the nav uses /praxisangebote instead. Pick one path and make all links match.
  • Homepage offer card 2 links to /workshops; the real route is /workshops-kurse.
  • /impressum, /datenschutz, /cookies, /sitemap — footer legal links, all missing. Legally required for a Swiss site.

Spelling / consistency Same word written differently across the site

  • “arbeitest du anderst” → correct German is “anders” (appears as a big heading on Ausbildung & Übersicht).
  • “Nummerologie” / “NUMMEROLOGIE” → correct German is “Numerologie” (one m). The nav already spells it right; the homepage, calendar & course slug don’t.
  • Homepage hero title is broken: Erforsche die<br> Systeme der .<br>Seele — there’s a stray “ . ” and odd line breaks. Should read “Erforsche die Systeme der Seele”.
  • “EIch bin der Titel des Kurses” (Aktueller Monatsworkshop hero) — typo + placeholder.

Factual / labelling

  • Homepage “Klient:innen-Stimme” shows “Theresia & Axel Jansen · Klient:innen seit 2024” — the founders are labelled as their own clients. Replace with a real client quote, or relabel as a founders’ statement.
  • Footer tagline is still Lorem ipsum.
  • Images everywhere are placeholder/Placeholder-*.jpg with inconsistent capitalisation (Placeholder_1 vs placeholder-3) — needs the real photo set with a single naming convention.
